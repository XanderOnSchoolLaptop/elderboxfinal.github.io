Basic Edition

This is your specialized Elderbox for you elder.
It includes clothing, gadgets, a cane, and some cool grips and feet for that cane.

Is this box right for your elder?

The Basic Edition Elderbox is a box meant for the average elder. The first month costs $150 dollars and comes with a special edition Elderbox cane and a colored grip of your choice. For the next few months there are random gadgets, clothing and things to put on the cane. By the end of the first year your elder will have a lot of cool useful gadgets and a fully customized cane. If this sounds right the price is $150 for the first month and $50 for the rest.
